## Why Slow Rules (Website Performance)
1. Fewer HTTP Requests
2. Use a CDN
3. Expires/Cache-Control Header
4. Gzip
5. Stylesheets at Top
6. Scripts at Bottom
7. CSS Expressions
8. External JS/CSS
9. Fewer DNS Lookups
10. Minify JS/CSS
11. Redirects
12. Duplicate Scripts
13. ETags
14. Cacheable Ajax
